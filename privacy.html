Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is handled by this application ("the App"), a private, non-commercial integration with the Oura Ring API.

Purpose

The App exists solely for personal use. It accesses the developer's own Oura Ring data to generate personal health summaries and insights. It is not offered as a product or service to any other person.

Data Collected

Through the official Oura API (read-only OAuth 2.0), the App may retrieve daily summary metrics including readiness, sleep, activity, heart rate, HRV, temperature deviation, and basic account identifiers such as Oura user ID and email address. No raw biometric sensor streams, GPS data, or payment information are collected.

How Data Is Used

Data is used exclusively to generate personal wellness summaries and reports for the developer's own reflection and tracking. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Data retrieved from the Oura API is stored locally on the developer's own devices and infrastructure. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services beyond what is required for the Oura OAuth 2.0 authentication flow.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as it is useful for personal tracking purposes. The developer may delete stored data at any time.

Third-Party Access

No third party has access to any data retrieved through this App. The App does not integrate with analytics services, advertising networks, or any other external platforms.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the developer via the information listed on this site.
